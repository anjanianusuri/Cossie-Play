Cossie Play Parties

Party Time:

First Saturday of every month, 6pm - 11pm

Adults:

$15

Children (6-17):

$10

Children (0-5):

Free

Include:

One non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick.

On the first Saturday of every month we hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the town hall. Entry is $15 for adults, $10 for ages 6-17 and kids under 6 are free. There is always a live DJ at the party (who takes requests) and dancing is common (but not required).

Party Guidelines

Alcohol

Served at the bar(18+ only). No intoxicated persons will be served, but will be escorted to a taxi – paid by the party-goer.

Fees

Paid at the door. Cash or EFTPOS accepted. Credit card payments attract 2% charge.
